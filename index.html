Introduction

Artificial Intelligence or AI is the hottest buzzword in coding and electronics right now.  It seems like everything from Google’s search results to household appliances has AI now.  Some AI, like spell check, has been part of our lives for a long time now.  The newer, flashier use of artificial intelligence is generative ai or GenAI.  GenAI uses internet content to train machine learning to then create new content.
GenAI has been a source of entertainment and discourse since programs like Dall-E 3 and ChatGPT were released to the public.  Programmers have been trying to use computers to make art since the early days of computing (Ahuja & Kumar, 2024).  Now that GenAI is able to produce images and text with a simple prompt from the user, we, as a society, are faced with many questions.
